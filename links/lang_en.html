Auditorias Energéticas

Andreu Manteniment Industrial carries out energy audits in order to optimize resources and improve energy efficiency in your facilities.

In addition to improve the quality of your installation, we can help you to extend the life of your equipment and reduce your expenses in electrical energy.

Initially we do a customized study of the state of the installation using network analyzers. After analyzing the consumption of the equipment we made a report detailing the possible improvements and making an estimate report of the economic savings that it would entail.

We make use of progressive starters or frequency inverters, so we can adjust the consumption depending on the load of the equipment, adapting it to the circumstances of the production process.

So you get a fast payback of energy-saving equipment.

Thermographic reports

We offer maintenance services, but we don't just fix and solve faults, we provide a predictive and preventive service through the study of thermal cameras images.

Thermographic studies are an important tool for preventive and predictive maintenance of installations of all types such as: motors, transformers, electrical panels, furnances, cold rooms, etc.

Thermographical reports allows the localization of hot spots that indicate the presence of operational defects in the equipment before the fault occurs. In this way, preventive maintenance of the installations can be carried out.

Applications

  • Detection of anomalies in the system without interrupting the production process.
    Our customers can save production costs by minimizing the number of production stops for equipment maintenance. Preventive maintenance can be done more frequently as it does not intervene in the manufacturing process with the consequent improvement of performance.
  • Location with exactness and anticipation of problems.
    The accuracy of these thermographic systems allows us to quickly and accurately determine the focus of the problem. The equipment such as bearings, motors, pumps, etc., with a substantial temperature increase can indicate the malfunction of the equipment and thus anticipate future breakdowns.
  • Decrease maintenance actions and reduce repair time.
    These preventive maintenance improvements are positively reflected in production and maintenance costs, optimizing the resources we have and the time spent in maintenance.
  • Extended life of equipment.
    The life of the equipment is directly influenced by the operation and maintenance done, so an efficient maintenance allows us to extend the life of our equipment.
  • Efficient energy use.
    The consumption in the refrigeration / heating equipment is diminished acting on the causes of the losses of cold or heat.
    In this way both energy savings and lower environmental impact are achieved.
  • Get discounts on insurance policy
    The efficiency of thermographic studies, the vast majority of insurance companies value positively that your company has a maintenance program with thermographic analysis.

Documentation

With each thermographic study we elaborate a complete report of the state of all the points of the installation, pointing out the most critical points of the installation.
